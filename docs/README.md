## 🚀 What is Birjuram.ai?

Birjuram.ai is your personal AI-powered career mentor, designed to guide you from ambition to achievement. In today's fast-paced world, navigating a career path can be complex. Birjuram.ai simplifies this journey by providing the structure, tools, and personalized feedback you need to succeed.

It's not just a planner; it's a dynamic mentor that learns about you, quizzes your knowledge, and rewards your progress, making skill acquisition engaging and effective.

## ✨ Core Features

Birjuram.ai is built around an intelligent, interactive, and rewarding learning ecosystem.

### 1. AI-Powered Counseling & Roadmap Generation

- **Personalized Counseling:** Get 1-on-1 guidance from our AI mentor. Ask complex questions about career changes, skill-gap analysis, or "what to learn next." The AI provides detailed, empathetic, and expert-level advice based on your unique profile.
- **Dynamic Roadmaps:** Forget static plans. Birjuram.ai generates a **structured learning roadmap** tailored to your goal (e.g., "Become a Data Scientist"). The roadmap is broken down into stages, modules, and actionable tasks.
- **Adaptive Learning:** The roadmap isn't set in stone. As you complete quizzes and update your skills, the AI will suggest adjustments, helping you focus on weak areas or fast-track through topics you already know.

### 2. Stage-Wise Quiz Generation

- **Knowledge Checkpoints:** At the end of each roadmap stage or module, the AI generates a **custom quiz** to validate your understanding of the topics.
- **Multiple Formats:** Quizzes include multiple-choice, short-answer, and even code-snippet analysis to test practical application.
- **Instant Feedback:** Pass a quiz to unlock the next stage. If you struggle, the AI provides detailed explanations for incorrect answers and recommends specific resources to review.

### 3. Gamified Reward System

- **Reward Points ($POINTS_CURRENCY_NAME):** We make learning addictive. Earn points for every action you take:
  - Completing a roadmap task.
  - Passing a quiz on the first try.
  - Completing an entire roadmap.
- **Quiz Streaks:** Maintain a **daily or weekly streak** of correctly answered quizzes to earn bonus point multipliers. Don't break the chain!
- **Leaderboards & Badges:** See how you stack up against other learners on a weekly leaderboard. Earn special badges for major achievements, like "Roadmap Complete," "Interview Pro," or "7-Day Streak."

### 4. Productivity & Interview Prep

- **Productivity Tracking:** Set daily, weekly, and long-term goals. Our dashboard helps you break down large goals into manageable tasks and visualizes your progress.
- **AI-Driven Mock Interviews:** Once your roadmap is complete, practice for technical and behavioral interviews with our advanced AI. Receive instant, detailed feedback on your answers, communication style, and key talking points.

## 💻 Tech Stack

Birjuram.ai is built with a modern, scalable, and AI-first technology stack:

- **Frontend:** React / Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Python (FastAPI)
- **Database:** Firestore for core data access and storage
- **AI/ML:**
  - **Core LLM:** Google Gemini API (for counseling & generation)
- **DevOps:** Docker, Kubernetes, GitHub Actions (CI/CD)
- **Cloud/ Deployment:** Google Cloud Run
